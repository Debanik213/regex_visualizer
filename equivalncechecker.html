Core Theory

Equivalence of Languages

Two regular expressions R₁ and R₂ are said to be equivalent if they describe exactly the same language.

"Even though two patterns look different on the page, they may accept the exact same infinite set of strings from the alphabet Σ."

Common Identities

  • R + R = R (Idempotence)

  • (R*)* = R* (Closure Property)

  • R(S+T) = RS+RT (Distributivity)
